A Very Kerbal Christmas
=======================
Contact: Nazari1382 on the Kerbal Space Program Forum


Includes sled, skids, and a decoupler for your sled's cargo.

Installation:
Place both the MP_Nazari and Firespitter folder in your GameData folder.



Also included is an updated copy (for KSP 0.90) of the Firespitter plugin by Snjo.

Firespitter Thread:

http://forum.kerbalspaceprogram.com/threads/24551-Firespitter-propeller-plane-and-helicopter-parts-v6-3-5-%28Sep-1st%29-for-KSP-0-24-2
